What happens next

—

We will review the outline you have provided and consider whether this is a matter we can assist with.

—

We will be in touch within two working days with a response. If your matter is urgent and you have not heard from us, you can contact us directly at hello@dawsonhouse.org.uk.

—

If we can help, we will set out clearly what that would involve and what it would cost. There is no commitment on either side until we have agreed a scope and you have confirmed you wish to proceed.

—

If the matter would be better handled elsewhere, we will tell you and, where we can, point you in the right direction.

We will be direct about whether we can help, and if we are not the right people, we will say so.
